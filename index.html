I belong to the Interfaces doctoral school of Paris-Saclay University. I work with CS Group, the Industrial Engineering Laboratory (LGI) at CentraleSupélec and the ESTIA-Research group.

Research and publications

The domains of my research include supporting design stages in Systems Engineering and Systems Architecture. In particular, I am intersted in Human Systems Integration (HSI) approaches to the design of complex sociotechnical systems. Such approaches include simulation, virtual protoypes, Scenario-Based Design (SBD), cognitive engineering and complex systems modeling. In this regard, I am a member of the FlexTech chair which is a long-term collaboration between both industrial and academic partners.
